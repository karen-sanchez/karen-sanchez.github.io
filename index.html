About Me

I am Karen, a designer and web developer based in NYC. I love what I do becasue it gives me the freedom to create and execute things that I or others simply imagine. A typical project for me includes drawing out ideas on paper, designing it on Photoshop, and then bringing the design to the web with different development tools. I enjoy being part of development every step of the way, because that is to me the most rewarding part of this job.

In my view, design and development are two of the most useful skills that anyone can have in today's world. Both fields can encourage us to become bolder, motivated, and to appreacite the beauty that even a website can have. Design offers me a way to express myself outside of words, to think creatively, and to not hesitate to try new things. Development allows me to solve different problems everyday, the more challenging, the more rewarding to solve it.

I love what I do, and when I am not doing that I can be found reading, writing, or dancing.
