About Me

I am Karen, a designer and web developer based in NYC. I am a web developer because it gives me the freedom to create and execute things that I or others simply imagine. Inspiration, design, and development are the three most important steps that I take when I do my work. A typical project for me includes drawing out ideas on paper, designing it on Photoshop, and then bringing the design to the web with different development tools. I enjoy being part of development every step of the way, because that is to me the most rewarding part of being a developer.
