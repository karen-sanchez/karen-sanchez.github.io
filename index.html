Why I chose web development

I started coding about a year ago, I began by teaching myself HTML and CSS, I did some Ruby as well. After a couple of months I decided that I wanted to take coding more seriously, that's when I quit my job and started an intensive course at a coding bootcamp. It was a tough first weeks, but after the heart-attack that JavaScript gave me was over I decided I was going to stick with my decision of learning how to program. I am glad I didn't quit. I don't even dislike JavaScript anymore, and now I am confident that I can learn anything I want, just like I did with Bootstrap, Skeleton, d3, Node.js, and Jade. Today, I couldn't be happier to be building my own apps with Rails and Node.js.
